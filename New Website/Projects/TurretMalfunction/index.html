Turret Malfunction

Turret Malfunction is a browser game I made for the 2020 GMTK Game Jam during lockdown, in collaboration with Adam.

We created it using Unity, and all the art was designed by Adam.

The Story

In a dystopian future, you operate one of the many city defense turrets, to protect your city from the Zombies.

However, your turret has malfunctioned, gone out of control, and with the zombies approaching fast, you have no escape - you must make the most of a bad situation and continue to operate your out of control turret.

The Gameplay

The premise is simple, shoot the zombies before they reach you. However, you cannot control your turret's direction, it just keeps spinning. Furthermore, you only get one bullet at a time, adding an extra level of challenge to the game. Every wave (10 points), the speed of the zombies gradually increases.
